About us

Welcome to

Adoring LLC

Adoring LLC is a premier supplier and exporter of high-quality dried prunes, based in Uzbekistan. Our company specializes in sourcing, storing, and exporting premium prunes that meet the strictest international standards, including EU regulations. With a strong focus on quality and sustainability, Adoring LLC has established itself as a trusted partner for clients worldwide.

  • Our Mission

    At Adoring LLC, our mission is to provide the global market with the finest dried prunes, grown and processed with care, while ensuring the highest levels of quality and sustainability. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients by consistently delivering products that exceed expectations.

  • Our Vision

    To become a globally recognized leader in the dried prune industry, renowned for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Core Values

  • •

    Quality Assurance: We adhere to stringent quality control measures at every stage of our process, ensuring that our prunes meet the highest international standards.

  • •

    Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, from sourcing to shipping, ensuring minimal impact on the environment.

  • •

    Customer-Centric Approach: Our clients’ needs and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We aim to provide tailored solutions that meet specific market demands.

  • •

    Innovation: We continuously invest in modern processing and storage technologies to enhance product quality and extend shelf life.

What We Offer

Adoring LLC specializes in providing:

  • •

    Dried Prunes: Sourced from the best orchards in Uzbekistan, our prunes are carefully processed to maintain their natural flavor, nutritional value, and quality. We offer prunes in various sizes and packaging options to meet the needs of different markets

  • •

    Custom Packaging: We offer flexible packaging solutions to meet the specific requirements of our clients, from bulk packaging for industrial use to retail-ready options.

  • •

    Global Export: With experience in exporting to over 15 countries, we ensure efficient and timely delivery to clients worldwide, adhering to all necessary international regulations and certifications.

Processing & Quality Control

Our prunes go through a rigorous process that includes:

  • •

    Sourcing: Handpicked from carefully selected orchards known for producing high-quality prunes.

  • •

    Processing: Using modern, hygienic facilities to clean, dry, and package prunes, while maintaining their natural properties.

  • •

    Storage: Prunes are stored in temperature-controlled environments to preserve freshness and quality.

  • •

    Quality Assurance:Every batch is subject to strict quality checks, ensuring compliance with EU and other international standards.

Our Global Reach

With clients in over 15 countries, Adoring LLC has established a strong presence in the international market. Our expertise in global logistics ensures that our products reach our customers in optimal condition, no matter where they are located.
