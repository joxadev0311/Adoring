Welcome to

Adoring LLC

Adoring is situated in the picturesque Fergana Valley, Uzbekistan, a historic region renowned for its exceptional dried prunes. As a leading exporter, we manage every step of the process, from sourcing the finest plums to storing, reprocessing, and exporting our high-quality dried prunes. Our operations include premium plums sourced not only from the Fergana Valley but also from Kyrgyzstan, ensuring a diverse and top-notch product range.

About product packages

Our products can be purchased in 2 different packages.

For importers

For local manufacturers

Type 1 - customers who receive raw materials from us. These customers mainly process themselves in their countries after importing the product from us. They receive them because they are more perfect in their own production or because they are recycled in their own types. (And they are delivered in bags weighing 20 kg.)

Type 2 - customers get ready-made products from us. And to them, at 5/10 kg, we give the product with our label. Here we can tell you how many boxes can go on top of a paddock.
